End 1/4 | You: 0 CPU: 0 | Stones left: 4
Drag down from your stone (at the bottom) and release to throw — drag distance sets power, drag angle sets curl/direction. Slide your stone as close to the center of the house (the target circles) as possible. You and the CPU alternate throws; after all stones are thrown, the player(s) with stones closest to the center score points for that end. Most points after 4 ends wins!
