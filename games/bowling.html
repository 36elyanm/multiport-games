Bowling
Frame 1 — Roll 1 Aim, then tap to charge power
Aim: drag left/right on the lane (or use ◀ ▶ buttons) to set your throw angle. Power: tap/click to start charging the meter, tap again to lock in power and roll the ball — timing matters! The ball curves based on your aim angle and knocks down pins. Bowl all 10 frames; strikes 🎳 and spares get bonus scoring just like real bowling.
