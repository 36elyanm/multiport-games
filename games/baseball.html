Home Run Derby
Inning 1/3 | Score: 0 | Outs: 0/3
Watch the pitch come toward the plate and click / tap the canvas (or press Spacebar) at the right moment to swing! Perfect timing as the ball crosses the plate = Home Run. Slightly early/late = Double, Single or Foul. Way off = Miss (strike). Three strikes = an out, three outs ends your inning. Survive 3 innings and rack up the highest score!
