Klondike Solitaire
Click Stock to draw • Click a card to select, then click destination to move
How to play: Build tableau columns in alternating colors descending (e.g. red 9 on black 10). Move sequences or single cards onto another tableau pile, or to the foundations (top-right) building up by suit from Ace to King. Click the Stock pile to draw a card to the Waste pile. Tap/click a card to select it (highlighted), then tap/click a destination pile to move it there. An empty tableau column accepts only a King. Win by building all four foundations from Ace to King!
