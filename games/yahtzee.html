How to play: Each turn you get up to 3 rolls of 5 dice. After the first roll, click dice to hold them (highlighted gold) before rolling again. When ready, click any available category in the scorecard to record your score there (highlighted categories show your best current option). Categories highlighted in red are good potential choices for your current dice. The game lasts 13 rounds — once every category is filled, your final total is shown. Score smartly: each category can only be used once!
