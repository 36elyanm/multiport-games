Guide your character through the maze, eating all the small dots to clear the level. Move with arrow keys, WASD, on-screen D-pad, or by swiping on the maze. The big flashing power pellets turn the ghosts blue for a few seconds — eat them for bonus points! Touching a normal ghost costs you a life; lose all 3 lives and it's game over. Clear all dots to advance to the next, faster level.
