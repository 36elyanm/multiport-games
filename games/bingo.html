How to play: You're given a 5×5 card with a free center space. Click Draw Number (or enable auto-draw) to call a random number — it's announced and added to the history list. Click any matching number on your card to mark it. Complete a full row, column, or diagonal of marked cells (the free space counts as marked) to get BINGO! Click New Card any time to generate a fresh card and reset the game.
