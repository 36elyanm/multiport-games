Darts — 501
Player: 501 | CPU: 501 | Dart 1/3
Classic 501: each player starts at 501 and the first to reduce their score to exactly 0 — finishing on a DOUBLE — wins. Click/tap and drag down from the dartboard to aim and set power, then release to throw. You get 3 darts per turn, then the CPU throws. Singles, doubles (outer ring), triples (inner ring) and the bullseye (50) all count.
