Final score

You play Black, CPU plays White. Tap a highlighted square to place a disc — any opponent discs trapped in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) between your new disc and another of your discs get flipped to your color. You must place where you can flip at least one disc; if you have no legal move your turn is skipped. The game ends when neither side can move — whoever has the most discs wins!
