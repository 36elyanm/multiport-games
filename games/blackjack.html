You
How to play: Enter a bet and click Deal — you and the dealer each get two cards (one dealer card hidden). Hit to take another card, Stand to end your turn, or Double to double your bet and take exactly one more card. The dealer reveals their hidden card and must hit until reaching 17 or more. Closest to 21 without going over wins. Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card on the deal) pays 3:2. Your chip balance persists between hands — click New Hand to continue.
