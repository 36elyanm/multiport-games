Your turn — tap tokens in one pile, then confirm
Piles of tokens sit on the table. On your turn, tap one or more tokens from a single pile (they highlight), then press Remove Selected to take them away — you must remove at least one token. In Misère mode (default) the player forced to take the last token loses; in Normal mode taking the last token wins. The CPU calculates the optimal move using nim-sum strategy — it plays perfectly when a winning move exists!
