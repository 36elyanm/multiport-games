Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Multiport Games is a free browser-game site. This page explains, in plain language, what information we collect and how it's used. We collect as little as possible.

What we collect

  • Account info (optional): if you create an account, we store your username, email address, and a securely hashed version of your password (we never store your actual password).
  • Bug reports (optional): if you report a bug, we store the game name, your description, and an email address only if you choose to provide one — so we can follow up.
  • High scores & preferences: stored only in your browser's local storage on your own device. They are never sent to our servers.
  • Push notification subscriptions (admin only): if the site owner enables notifications, a device subscription token is stored so we can deliver alerts. This applies only to the admin dashboard, not regular players.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your information with third parties.
  • We don't run advertising trackers or third-party analytics.
  • We don't use cookies for tracking — sessions are kept locally in your browser.

How we use your information

Account details are used only to let you sign in and (in the future) save your progress. Bug report details are used only to find and fix problems with the games. Nothing is used for marketing.

Where data is stored

Data is stored securely using Cloudflare's infrastructure (D1 database), which encrypts data in transit via HTTPS.

Children's privacy

Multiport Games is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what's needed for an optional account (username/email), and we encourage younger players to ask a parent or guardian before creating an account or submitting an email address.

Your choices

You can play every game on this site without creating an account or submitting any personal information. If you'd like your account or bug report data removed, contact us using the bug report form and mention "delete my data" along with the email you used.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
